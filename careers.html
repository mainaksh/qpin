As we scale from 100 qubits (Quantum Volume ≥ 2^20, AQu ≥ 50) by 2028 to 100,000 qubits by 2040, we're building a world-class team to drive trillion-dollar innovations in medicine, energy, and sustainability. Join us to work with global experts from MIT, Caltech, and Harvard, and shape the quantum future.

Our Perks: Competitive USD salaries, equity options, comprehensive health insurance, remote/hybrid work, professional development (conferences, training), relocation assistance, and impact-driven culture with UN SDG alignment.
