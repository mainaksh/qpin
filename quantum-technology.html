Quantum Technology: Pioneering the Future

Qpin Systems’ 100-qubit fluxonium-based FTQC, with ≥50 Algorithmic Qubits, sets the stage for 100,000 qubits by 2040, delivering unmatched performance for exascale simulations.

Our Quantum Breakthrough

Qpin Systems is developing a 100-qubit fluxonium-based Fault-Tolerant Quantum Computer (FTQC) by 2028, with a Quantum Volume ≥ 2^20 and Algorithmic Qubits (AQu) ≥ 50, surpassing industry leaders like Google’s Willow (T1 ~68 μs) and IBM’s Condor (1Q fidelity ~99.9%). Our innovative use of fluxonium qubits, optimized for ultra-high anharmonicity (α > 1 GHz) and superior coherence times, positions us to lead the quantum revolution.

With an initial ₹3,000 Crore investment, we will scale to 500 qubits by 2030, 10,000+ by 2035, and 100,000 by 2040, securing ₹30,000 Crore through milestone-driven phases. Our technology enables quantum-accelerated algorithms for hyper-optimized simulations in quantum medicines, EV batteries, nuclear fusion, artificial leaves, hyper-yield seeds, and river-cleaning enzymes.

Why Fluxonium? Fluxonium qubits offer superior anharmonicity and coherence, enabling faster, error-resilient gates that outpace traditional superconducting qubits, paving the way for fault-tolerant quantum supremacy with ≥50 Algorithmic Qubits.

Technical Specifications

  • Algorithmic Qubits (AQu): ≥ 50, enabling a broad range of high-impact quantum algorithms for practical applications.
  • Coherence Time: T1 ≥ 1 ms, significantly exceeding Google’s Willow (T1 ~68 μs).
  • Gate Fidelity: 1Q gate fidelity ≥ 99.998%, surpassing IBM’s Condor (~99.9%).
  • Error Rate: Logical error rate p_L < 1e-6 using d=7 QLDPC/4D codes.
  • Fabrication: 300 mm wafer-scale with EBL < 5 nm, JJ uniformity σ/μ ≤ 1.5%.
  • Control Systems: Cryo-CMOS for 10x wiring reduction, enhancing scalability.
  • Decoding: QLDPC decoding via GraphQEC/Astra, achieving <500 μs latency.
  • Qubit Design: EJ/EC = 5-20, EJ/EL = 20-100 for optimal performance.
